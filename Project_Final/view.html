Roti/Phulka With Curry

Our roti/phulka is a testament to traditional craftsmanship, prepared with care and expertise. Each roti/phulka is lovingly hand-rolled and cooked on an open flame, resulting in a light and fluffy texture that complements our flavorful curries perfectly. Our curries are a tantalizing blend of aromatic spices, fresh vegetables, and tender meats (optional), simmered to perfection. From creamy lentil curries to spicy vegetable masalas, each curry is a symphony of flavors that will transport you to culinary bliss. The combination of warm roti/phulka and rich, flavorful curry is a match made in heaven, creating a wholesome and satisfying meal.

Vegetarian Biryani

Savor the exquisite flavors of our vegetarian biryani, a culinary masterpiece that will transport you to the heart of aromatic indulgence. Our vegetarian biryani is a symphony of fragrant basmati rice, perfectly cooked and layered with an assortment of fresh vegetables, aromatic spices, and herbs. Each spoonful is a burst of flavors, as the spices infuse into the tender rice and vegetables, creating a harmonious blend of textures and tastes. Whether you prefer a mild and aromatic biryani or a spicy and robust one, our vegetarian biryani is tailored to satisfy every palate. Accompanied by raita and garnished with crispy fried onions and fresh herbs, our biryani is a feast for both the eyes and the taste buds.

Non-Vegetarian Biryani

Indulge in the irresistible flavors of our non-vegetarian biryani, a culinary symphony that will tantalize your taste buds. Our non-vegetarian biryani is a masterpiece crafted with fragrant basmati rice, tender meats (such as chicken, lamb, or fish), and a medley of aromatic spices and herbs. Each layer of rice is infused with the flavors of succulent meat, creating a harmony of textures and tastes that will leave you craving for more. Whether you prefer a subtly spiced biryani or a fiery and robust one, our non-vegetarian biryani is expertly prepared to cater to all spice preferences. Accompanied by raita and garnished with fried onions and fresh herbs, our biryani is a culinary delight that captures the essence of traditional Indian cuisine.
